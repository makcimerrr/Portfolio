## Website Features
Our website is built using [Astro.build](https://astro.build), a modern framework for building fast and optimized web applications. The site includes the following sections:

### 1. Home
![Opengraph](https://i.goopics.net/f4xibp.png)

An overview of our services, showcasing our expertise and commitment to quality.

### 2. Portfolio

A curated collection of our past projects, highlighting the diversity and quality of our work. Each project includes:
- Project description
- Technologies used
- Visuals and screenshots

### 3. Templates

A selection of customizable templates that clients can choose from for their projects. These templates serve as a starting point, which we can further tailor to specific needs.

### 4. Pricing Plans

Clear and transparent pricing plans to suit different project sizes and budgets. Our pricing structure includes:
- Basic Plan: Ideal for small projects and startups
- Standard Plan: Perfect for growing businesses
- Premium Plan: Comprehensive solutions for larger projects

### 5. Blog

Regular updates and insights on web design trends, best practices, and our latest projects. Our blog aims to educate and inspire both clients and fellow developers.

## Getting Started

To get started with KreaLabs, follow these simple steps:

1. **Visit Our Website**: Explore our services, portfolio, and blog at [KreaLabs](https://krealabs.fr/).
2. **Choose a Template**: Select a template that best fits your project needs.
3. **Contact Us**: Reach out through our contact form to discuss your project requirements and get a custom quote.
4. **Start Your Project**: Once we finalize the details, we'll begin working on your project, keeping you informed throughout the process.
